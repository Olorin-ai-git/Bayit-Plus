# Judaism Section

The Judaism section in Bayit+ provides a comprehensive hub for Jewish content, learning resources, and community connection.

## What You Will Find

The Judaism section includes:

- Torah learning videos and audio shiurim
- Daily Daf Yomi and Parasha content
- Jewish calendar with holiday information
- Zmanim (halachic times) for your location
- Community directory for local resources
- Shabbat mode for automated scheduling

## Accessing the Judaism Section

To access Jewish content:

1. Open the main menu
2. Select Judaism
3. Browse available categories

The section adapts to your preferences and location to show relevant content.

## Personalized Content

Bayit+ learns your interests over time:

- Shiurim from rabbis you follow appear first
- Content matching your learning level is highlighted
- Holiday-specific content appears before each chag
- Location-based zmanim and community resources

## Daily Features

Start each day with Jewish content:

- Morning ritual with weather, news, and inspiration
- Daily Tehillim and learning reminders
- Yahrzeit notifications for family members
- Shabbat and holiday countdown timers

## Learning Tracks

Follow structured learning programs:

- Daf Yomi with multiple maggidei shiur
- Weekly Parasha with commentary
- Mishnah Yomit and Halacha Yomit
- Holiday preparation courses

## Community Connection

Connect with the Jewish community through:

- Synagogue and minyan finders
- Kosher restaurant directory
- Jewish organization listings
- Event calendars for your area
