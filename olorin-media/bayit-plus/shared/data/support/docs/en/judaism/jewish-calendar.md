# Jewish Calendar

The Jewish Calendar in Bayit+ keeps you connected to the rhythm of Jewish life with dates, holidays, and events.

## Viewing the Hebrew Date

The current Hebrew date is always visible:

1. Open the Judaism section
2. The Hebrew date appears at the top
3. Tap it to see the full calendar view

The calendar displays both Hebrew and Gregorian dates together.

## Upcoming Holidays

See what Jewish holidays are approaching:

- Holidays appear on the calendar with descriptions
- Countdown timers show days until the next chag
- Holiday-specific content is featured automatically
- Preparation guides appear before major holidays

## Holiday Content

Each holiday includes dedicated resources:

- Laws and customs explanations
- Recipe videos for traditional foods
- Music and entertainment for the chag
- Children's content about the holiday
- Historical and educational documentaries

## Personal Events

Add your own events to the Jewish calendar:

1. Tap any date on the calendar
2. Select Add Event
3. Enter event details
4. Set reminder notifications

Events can be set to repeat annually on the Hebrew date.

## Yahrzeits

Track family yahrzeits with automatic reminders:

1. Go to Calendar settings
2. Select Yahrzeits
3. Add family member names and dates
4. Choose notification preferences

You will receive reminders before each yahrzeit.

## Omer Counter

During the Omer period:

- Daily count appears in the Judaism section
- Notifications remind you to count each night
- Track your counting streak
- Access shiurim about the significance of each day

## Syncing with Device Calendar

Export Jewish dates and your events to your device calendar for unified scheduling across all your apps.
