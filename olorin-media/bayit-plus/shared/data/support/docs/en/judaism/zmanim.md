# Zmanim

Bayit+ provides accurate halachic times (zmanim) based on your location for daily observance and prayer.

## Viewing Daily Zmanim

To see today's times:

1. Open the Judaism section
2. Zmanim appear on the main screen
3. Tap to view the full list of times

Times update automatically based on your current location.

## Available Zmanim

The following halachic times are displayed:

- Alos HaShachar (dawn)
- Earliest Tallit and Tefillin
- Netz HaChama (sunrise)
- Latest Shema (Magen Avraham)
- Latest Shema (Gra)
- Latest Shacharit
- Chatzot (midday)
- Mincha Gedola
- Mincha Ketana
- Plag HaMincha
- Shkiah (sunset)
- Tzeit HaKochavim (nightfall)
- Chatzot Layla (midnight)

## Shabbat Times

Special times for Shabbat include:

- Candle lighting (18 minutes before sunset by default)
- Shabbat ends (after three stars appear)
- Rabbeinu Tam time

Adjust candle lighting minutes in your settings based on your custom.

## Location Settings

Configure your location for accurate times:

1. Go to Settings
2. Select Location
3. Choose automatic detection or enter manually
4. Select your elevation if known

Elevation affects zmanim calculations, especially for mountainous areas.

## Zmanim Notifications

Set alerts for important times:

- Candle lighting reminders
- Mincha time notifications
- Shkiah warnings
- Custom alerts for any zman

Configure notification preferences in the Judaism settings.

## Calculation Methods

Select your preferred calculation method:

- Gra (Vilna Gaon)
- Magen Avraham
- Rabbeinu Tam
- Fixed minutes options

Different communities follow different shitot, and Bayit+ accommodates all major opinions.

## Widget Display

Add zmanim to your home screen for quick reference without opening the app.
