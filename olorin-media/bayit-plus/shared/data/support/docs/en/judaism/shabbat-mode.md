# Shabbat Mode

Shabbat Mode in Bayit+ automatically adjusts your streaming experience based on halachic times for your location.

## How Shabbat Mode Works

When enabled, Shabbat Mode:

- Pauses all streaming at candle lighting time
- Disables notifications during Shabbat
- Resumes functionality after Havdalah time
- Applies to all devices on your account

The mode activates and deactivates automatically without any action required.

## Enabling Shabbat Mode

To enable automatic Shabbat Mode:

1. Go to Settings
2. Select Judaism preferences
3. Toggle on Automatic Shabbat Mode
4. Confirm your location for accurate times

Location permissions allow precise zmanim calculation.

## Location Settings

Shabbat Mode uses your location to determine times:

- Set your primary location manually if preferred
- Travel mode adjusts when you visit other areas
- Choose your minhag for time calculations
- Select early candle lighting minutes if desired

## Holiday Mode

Shabbat Mode also activates for Yom Tov:

- Applies to all two-day holidays outside Israel
- Single-day application in Israel
- Covers Yom Kippur and other fast days
- Chol HaMoed streaming remains available

## Pre-Shabbat Reminders

Receive helpful notifications before Shabbat:

- Reminder 1 hour before candle lighting
- Reminder at plag hamincha
- Final reminder 18 minutes before sunset
- Custom reminder times available in settings

## Content Queue

Queue content to watch after Shabbat:

1. Add shows or movies to your Shabbat queue
2. Content is ready when Shabbat ends
3. Resume watching right after Havdalah

This feature helps you plan your Motzei Shabbat viewing.

## Manual Override

If needed, you can temporarily disable Shabbat Mode for specific situations through the Settings menu.
