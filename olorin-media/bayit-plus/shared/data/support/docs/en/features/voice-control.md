# Voice Control Guide

Navigate Bayit+ hands-free with our intelligent voice assistant.

## Activating Voice Control

### Wake Word
Say **"Hey Bayit"** to activate the voice assistant anywhere in the app.

### Manual Activation
- Tap the floating microphone button
- Press the microphone icon in search
- Use the voice button on your remote (TV)

### Keyboard Shortcut
Press **V** key on web or TV to activate voice mode.

## Voice Assistant Features

### Contextual Understanding
The assistant understands context:
- "Play it" - Plays the item you're viewing
- "Add this to my list" - Saves current title
- "More like this" - Similar recommendations

### Multi-Language Support
Speak in your preferred language:
- English
- Hebrew
- Spanish

Change voice language in **Settings** > **Voice** > **Language**.

### Natural Conversation
Ask follow-up questions:
1. "Find sci-fi movies"
2. "Only ones from this year"
3. "Something with action"
4. "Play the second one"

## Settings & Customization

### Voice Settings
Navigate to **Settings** > **Voice Control**:

- **Wake Word** - Enable/disable "Hey Bayit"
- **Voice Language** - Choose recognition language
- **Response Language** - Choose response language
- **Sound Effects** - Toggle activation sounds
- **Voice Volume** - Adjust response volume

### Privacy
- Voice data is processed securely
- Commands are not stored permanently
- Disable voice in Settings anytime
- Review voice activity in Privacy settings

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Speak Clearly** - Use natural, clear speech
2. **Reduce Noise** - Background noise affects accuracy
3. **Be Specific** - "Play Breaking Bad Season 1 Episode 1"
4. **Wait for Beep** - Speak after activation sound
5. **Use Keywords** - "Play", "Find", "Show", "Go to"

## Troubleshooting

### Voice Not Responding
1. Check microphone permissions
2. Ensure voice is enabled in Settings
3. Check internet connection
4. Try manual activation

### Wrong Interpretation
1. Speak more slowly
2. Reduce background noise
3. Try rephrasing the command
4. Use simpler commands

### Wake Word Not Detected
1. Say "Hey Bayit" clearly
2. Wait 1 second before speaking
3. Check wake word is enabled
4. May not work in noisy environments

### Microphone Issues
1. Grant microphone permission
2. Check device microphone works
3. Close other apps using mic
4. Restart the app

## Accessibility

Voice control helps users who:
- Have limited mobility
- Prefer hands-free navigation
- Find typing difficult
- Are multitasking

Combined with other accessibility features in **Settings** > **Accessibility**.
