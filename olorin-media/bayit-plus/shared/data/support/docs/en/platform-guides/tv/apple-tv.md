# Apple TV Setup

Experience Bayit+ on the big screen with the Apple TV app, featuring seamless integration with tvOS.

## Installation

1. Navigate to the **App Store** on your Apple TV
2. Search for **Bayit+** using the search tab
3. Select the app and click **Get**
4. Once installed, open Bayit+ from your home screen

## System Requirements

- Apple TV HD (4th generation) or Apple TV 4K
- tvOS 15.0 or later
- Active internet connection

## Signing In

### Using Your iPhone
1. Open Bayit+ on Apple TV
2. Select "Sign in with iPhone"
3. Hold your iPhone near the Apple TV
4. Follow the prompts on your iPhone to complete sign-in

### Manual Sign-In
1. Select "Sign in with Email"
2. Enter your credentials using the on-screen keyboard
3. Use the Siri Remote to navigate

## Siri Remote Navigation

### Basic Controls
- **Touch Surface:** Swipe to navigate, click to select
- **Menu Button:** Go back or access options
- **Play/Pause Button:** Control playback
- **TV Button:** Return to home screen

### During Playback
- Swipe left/right to seek through content
- Click to show/hide playback controls
- Swipe down to access audio and subtitle options

## Siri Voice Commands

Press and hold the Siri button to use voice commands:
- "Play action movies"
- "Skip ahead 30 seconds"
- "Turn on subtitles"
- "What did they say?"
- "Search for family content"

## Features

### Continue Watching
Your viewing progress syncs across all devices. Pick up where you left off automatically.

### Top Shelf
Add Bayit+ to your top row for quick access to recommendations directly from the home screen.

### Profiles
Switch between family member profiles to get personalized recommendations for each viewer.

## Tips

- Use the Apple TV Remote app on your iPhone for easier text entry
- Enable home sharing for a connected household experience
- Adjust video quality in Settings if you have bandwidth limitations
