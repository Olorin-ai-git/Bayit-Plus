# Android App Setup

Get started with Bayit+ on your Android phone or tablet and enjoy a feature-rich streaming experience.

## Installation

1. Open the **Google Play Store** on your device
2. Search for **Bayit+**
3. Tap **Install** to download
4. Open the app and sign in with your account

## System Requirements

- Android 8.0 (Oreo) or later
- 2 GB RAM minimum
- 150 MB free storage space

## Key Features

### Google Assistant Integration
Control Bayit+ with your voice:
- "Hey Google, open Bayit+"
- "Hey Google, play movies on Bayit+"
- "Hey Google, pause"

### Chromecast Support
Stream to your TV using Chromecast:
1. Ensure your device and Chromecast are on the same Wi-Fi network
2. Start playing content in the app
3. Tap the Cast icon in the player
4. Select your Chromecast device

### Picture-in-Picture (PiP)
Watch videos while multitasking:
1. Start playing a video
2. Press the Home button or gesture
3. The video continues in a small window
4. Drag to reposition or tap to expand

### Offline Downloads
Save content for offline viewing:
1. Browse to your desired content
2. Tap the download button
3. Choose your preferred quality
4. Find downloads in the Library section

## Settings Configuration

- **Streaming Quality:** Settings > Video Quality
- **Download Location:** Settings > Storage
- **Data Saver:** Settings > Cellular Data
- **Notifications:** Settings > Notifications

## Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:
- **Storage:** Required for offline downloads
- **Microphone:** Optional, for voice search

## Tips

- Use fingerprint or face unlock for quick access
- Enable battery optimization exceptions for uninterrupted downloads
- Connect Bluetooth headphones for private listening
