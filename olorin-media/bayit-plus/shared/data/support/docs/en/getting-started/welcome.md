# Welcome to Bayit+

Welcome to Bayit+, your premium streaming destination for the best in live TV, movies, and on-demand content.

## What is Bayit+?

Bayit+ is a comprehensive streaming platform that brings you:

- **Live TV Channels** - Watch your favorite channels in real-time with crystal-clear quality
- **Video on Demand** - Access thousands of movies and TV shows whenever you want
- **Multi-Language Support** - Enjoy content in English, Hebrew, and Spanish
- **Voice Control** - Navigate hands-free using voice commands
- **Cross-Platform Access** - Watch on your phone, tablet, TV, or web browser

## Getting Started

To begin your Bayit+ experience:

1. **Create an Account** - Sign up with your email or phone number
2. **Choose a Plan** - Select the subscription that fits your needs
3. **Download the App** - Available on iOS, Android, Apple TV, Android TV, and web
4. **Start Watching** - Browse channels, search for content, and enjoy!

## Key Features

### Live TV
Access hundreds of live channels including news, sports, entertainment, and more. Use the channel guide to see what's on now and what's coming up.

### Video Library
Browse our extensive catalog of movies and TV series. Filter by genre, language, or release date to find exactly what you're looking for.

### Voice Assistant
Say "Hey Bayit" to activate voice control. Ask for recommendations, search for content, or navigate the app hands-free.

### Personalization
Create user profiles, save favorites, and get personalized recommendations based on your viewing history.
