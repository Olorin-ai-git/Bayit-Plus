# Creating Your Bayit+ Account

Welcome to Bayit+! Setting up your account takes just a few minutes and gives you access to thousands of hours of entertainment.

## Sign Up Methods

Bayit+ offers several convenient ways to create your account:

### Email Registration

1. Visit the Bayit+ app or website
2. Select "Create Account"
3. Enter your email address and create a password
4. Verify your email by clicking the link sent to your inbox
5. Complete your profile setup

### Phone Number Registration

1. Select "Sign Up with Phone"
2. Enter your mobile number with country code
3. Receive a verification code via SMS
4. Enter the code to verify your number
5. Set up your password and profile

### Google OAuth

1. Select "Continue with Google"
2. Choose your Google account from the popup
3. Grant Bayit+ permission to access basic profile information
4. Your account is created automatically using your Google credentials

### QR Code Sign Up (TV Devices)

For Apple TV and Android TV users:

1. Open the Bayit+ app on your TV
2. Select "Create Account" from the welcome screen
3. A unique QR code appears on your TV screen
4. Scan the code with your mobile device camera
5. Complete registration on your phone
6. Your TV automatically signs in once registration is complete

## After Registration

Once your account is created, you can:

- Choose your subscription plan
- Create additional user profiles
- Set up parental controls
- Personalize your content preferences
- Link additional devices to your account

## Account Security

Bayit+ protects your account with industry-standard encryption. We recommend enabling two-factor authentication in your account settings for additional security.
