# Quick Start Guide

Get up and running with Bayit+ in just a few minutes.

## Step 1: Download the App

Download Bayit+ from your device's app store:

- **iOS**: App Store
- **Android**: Google Play Store
- **Apple TV**: App Store (tvOS)
- **Android TV**: Google Play Store
- **Web**: Visit our website

## Step 2: Create Your Account

1. Open the Bayit+ app
2. Tap **Sign Up**
3. Enter your email address or phone number
4. Create a secure password
5. Verify your email or phone

## Step 3: Choose Your Subscription

Select the plan that works best for you:

| Plan | Features | Price |
|------|----------|-------|
| Basic | SD streaming, 1 device | Monthly |
| Standard | HD streaming, 2 devices | Monthly |
| Premium | 4K streaming, 4 devices, downloads | Monthly |

## Step 4: Set Up Your Profile

1. Add a profile picture (optional)
2. Select your preferred language
3. Choose content preferences
4. Enable parental controls if needed

## Step 5: Start Watching

### Browse Live TV
- Open the **Live** tab
- Scroll through channels or use the guide
- Tap a channel to start watching

### Explore On-Demand
- Open the **Browse** tab
- Use categories or search
- Tap any title for details
- Press **Play** to start

### Use Voice Control
- Say "Hey Bayit" or tap the microphone
- Try: "Show me action movies"
- Try: "What's on ESPN?"
- Try: "Play The Office"

## Tips for Best Experience

1. **Strong Internet** - For HD streaming, use at least 10 Mbps
2. **Wi-Fi Recommended** - Save mobile data by connecting to Wi-Fi
3. **Update Regularly** - Keep the app updated for new features
4. **Use Favorites** - Star channels and shows for quick access
5. **Try Voice** - Voice control is faster than typing

## Keyboard Shortcuts (Web/TV)

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| Space | Play/Pause |
| ← → | Seek backward/forward |
| ↑ ↓ | Volume up/down |
| F | Toggle fullscreen |
| M | Mute/unmute |
| G | Open channel guide |
