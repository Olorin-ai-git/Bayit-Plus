# Bayit+ Platform Overview

Bayit+ is a comprehensive streaming platform designed to bring entertainment to every screen in your life. Whether at home or on the go, Bayit+ delivers movies, TV shows, live channels, podcasts, and more.

## Core Features

- **On-Demand Content**: Thousands of movies and TV series available anytime
- **Live TV**: Real-time broadcast channels with electronic program guide
- **Podcasts**: Curated audio content across diverse topics
- **Radio**: Live streaming of radio stations
- **DVR Recording**: Record live content for later viewing
- **Kids Mode**: Safe, age-appropriate content for children
- **Offline Downloads**: Watch content without internet connection

## Supported Devices

### Mobile Devices
- **iOS**: iPhone and iPad running iOS 14 or later
- **Android**: Phones and tablets running Android 8.0 or later

### Television
- **Apple TV**: tvOS 14 or later
- **Android TV**: Android TV OS 9.0 or later
- **Smart TVs**: Samsung, LG, and other manufacturers with Android TV

### Web Browser
- **Desktop**: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge on Windows and macOS
- **Mobile Web**: Responsive experience on mobile browsers

### Automotive
- **Apple CarPlay**: Audio content playback while driving
- **Android Auto**: Podcast and radio streaming in your vehicle

## Cross-Device Experience

Your Bayit+ experience syncs seamlessly across all devices:

- Continue watching from where you left off
- Access your watchlist and favorites everywhere
- Unified search across all content types
- Consistent user profiles and preferences

## Content Library

Bayit+ offers content in multiple languages with subtitle support, ensuring accessibility for diverse audiences. Our library includes original productions, licensed content, and exclusive premieres.
