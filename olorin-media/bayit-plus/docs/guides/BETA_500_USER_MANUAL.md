## Understanding AI Credits

### What Are AI Credits?

AI credits are the currency for using AI-powered features in Bayit+. Each AI operation (dubbing, search, recommendations) consumes a certain number of credits based on usage.

### Your Credit Allocation

- **Initial Credits**: 5,000 credits
- **Duration**: 90 days from verification
- **Renewal**: Not renewable (Beta 500 is a one-time program)

### Credit Consumption Rates

| Feature | Cost | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| **Live Dubbing** | 1 credit/second | 60 credits for 1 minute of dubbed audio |
| **AI Search** | 0.5 credits/query | 2 searches = 1 credit |

- Watch **83+ minutes** of live-dubbed content, OR

## Available Features

### 1. Live Dubbing (AI Audio Translation)

**What it does**: Translates audio in real-time as you watch content

**How to use**:
1. Start playing any video content
2. Tap the **voice/language icon** in the player controls
3. Select **"Live Dubbing"**
4. Choose your preferred language
5. The audio will be translated in real-time

**Credits**: 1 credit per second of dubbed audio

**Platforms**: Web, iOS, Android, tvOS

**Tips**:
- Live dubbing works best with clear, single-speaker audio

**How to use**:

**Platforms**: Web, iOS, Android, tvOS

- Use descriptive, natural language queries
- Be specific about what you're looking for
- Try semantic searches like "content that makes me laugh" or "documentaries about technology"

### 3. AI Recommendations

## Using Your Credits

### How Credits Are Deducted

Credits are deducted **automatically** when you use AI features:

**Live Dubbing**:
- Credits deduct every 30 seconds (checkpoint system)
- If you run out of credits mid-session, dubbing stops automatically
- You'll receive a notification when credits are depleted

**AI Search & Recommendations**:
- Credits deduct immediately when you request the feature
- If insufficient credits, you'll see a warning message

### Credit Checkpoints (Live Dubbing)

To ensure fair usage and prevent over-consumption, live dubbing uses a **checkpoint system**:
- Every 30 seconds, credits are deducted for usage
- If your balance is low, you'll receive a warning
- Sessions automatically end when credits reach zero

### Session Management

**Starting a session**:
- System checks if you have sufficient credits
- Session begins once confirmed

**During a session**:
- Credits deduct at 30-second intervals
- Low balance warnings appear at 500 credits
- Critical warnings appear at 100 credits

**Ending a session**:
- Final credit deduction occurs
- Remaining balance is displayed

## Credit Warnings

The system provides automatic warnings to help you manage your credits:

### Low Balance Warning

**Triggers at**: 500 credits remaining (10% of total)

**What you'll see**:
- Yellow warning badge on credit widget
- Notification: *"Warning: Credit balance is low"*
- Suggestion to upgrade (post-beta)

**What to do**:
- Continue using features conservatively
- Prioritize your most important AI features
- Check your balance regularly

### Critical Balance Warning

**Triggers at**: 100 credits remaining (2% of total)

**What you'll see**:
- Red critical badge on credit widget
- Notification: *"Critical: Running low on credits"*
- Estimated time remaining for live dubbing

**What to do**:
- Use features sparingly
- Avoid starting long dubbing sessions
- Prepare for service interruption when credits run out

### Zero Balance

**What happens**:
- AI features become unavailable
- You'll see: *"No AI credits remaining"*
- All AI-powered features are disabled

**What to do**:
- Beta 500 credits are not renewable during the beta period
- Contact support if you believe there's an error
- Wait for the public release for additional credit options

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### "Beta program is full"

**Problem**: Can't sign up - message says 500/500 slots filled

**Solution**:
- The beta program has reached capacity
- Join the waitlist to be notified of openings
- Occasionally, inactive users are removed, freeing up slots

#### "Verification link expired"

**Problem**: Email verification link doesn't work

**Solution**:
- Verification links expire after 24 hours
- Return to the signup page and register again
- Check your spam folder for the verification email
- Contact support if you continue having issues

#### "Email already registered"

**Problem**: Can't sign up with your email address

**Solution**:
- You've already signed up with this email
- Check your email for the verification link
- If you've lost the verification email, contact support

#### "Insufficient credits" error

**Problem**: Can't use a feature despite having credits

**Solution**:
- Check your actual balance (may be lower than expected)
- Some features require minimum credits (e.g., 30 credits for 30-second dubbing)
- Refresh the app to sync your balance
- Contact support if balance is incorrect

#### Live dubbing stops unexpectedly

**Problem**: Dubbing cuts out during playback

**Possible causes**:
1. **Credits depleted**: Check balance - you may have run out
2. **Network issue**: Check internet connection
3. **Session timeout**: Sessions end after 10 minutes of inactivity
4. **Content incompatibility**: Some content may not support live dubbing

**Solution**:
- Verify credit balance
- Check network connection
- Restart the dubbing session
- Try different content

#### Credits not updating

**Problem**: Balance doesn't reflect recent usage

**Solution**:
- Force-close and restart the app
- Wait 30 seconds for checkpoint sync
- Check for app updates
- Contact support if issue persists

#### Can't access Beta 500 features

**Problem**: AI features not available despite being verified

**Solution**:
1. Verify your email was confirmed (check for confirmation page)
2. Log out and log back in to refresh account status
3. Check that you're using the correct account
4. Ensure app is updated to latest version
5. Contact support with your user ID

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: How many people are in Beta 500?**
A: Exactly 500 beta testers. The program is limited to ensure high-quality testing and support.

**Q: How long does Beta 500 last?**
A: Your access is valid for **90 days** from the date you verify your email.

**Q: Can I get more credits?**
A: Beta 500 is a one-time allocation of 5,000 credits. Additional credits are not available during the beta period. After the public launch, credit packages will be available.

**Q: What happens after 90 days?**
A: Your Beta 500 access expires. You'll be notified about transition options to the public release with subscription pricing.

**Q: Are my credits transferable?**
A: No, Beta 500 credits are tied to your account and cannot be transferred to other users.

**Q: Can I share my Beta 500 access?**
A: No, Beta 500 access is non-transferable and for individual use only.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Which platforms support Beta 500?**
A: All Bayit+ platforms:
- Web (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge)
- iOS (iPhone, iPad)
- Android (phones, tablets)
- tvOS (Apple TV 4K and later)

**Q: Does live dubbing work offline?**
A: No, live dubbing requires an active internet connection for real-time AI processing.

**Q: What languages are supported for live dubbing?**
A: Current support includes:
- English, Spanish, Hebrew, Chinese, French, Italian, Hindi, Tamil, Bengali, Japanese
- Additional languages coming based on beta feedback

**Q: How accurate is live dubbing?**
A: Accuracy is typically 85-95% depending on:
- Audio clarity
- Speaker accent
- Background noise
- Technical terminology

**Q: Can I pause and resume a dubbing session?**
A: Yes, pausing the video pauses dubbing. Resume to continue. Credits only deduct during active dubbing.

### Credit Questions

**Q: How can I see my credit history?**
A: Detailed credit transaction history is coming in a future update. Currently, you can see your remaining balance and total usage.

**Q: What if I run out of credits early?**
A: Beta 500 credits are not renewable during the beta period. Use features conservatively. Contact support if you believe there's an error in your balance.

**Q: Do credits expire?**
A: Yes, credits expire 90 days after email verification, along with your Beta 500 access.

**Q: Can I buy more credits during the beta?**
A: No, additional credits are not available during Beta 500. The public release will offer credit packages.

### Privacy & Data Questions

**Q: What data is collected during the beta?**
A: We collect:
- Feature usage statistics (anonymous)
- Credit consumption patterns
- Error logs and performance data
- Feedback submissions

We do NOT collect:
- Content of your searches (only that a search occurred)
- Audio/video you watch (only duration of dubbing sessions)
- Personal conversations or messages

**Q: Is my feedback confidential?**
A: Feedback is used internally for product improvement. Your identity is kept confidential unless you explicitly consent to being quoted.

**Q: Can I opt out of data collection?**
A: Basic usage data is required for Beta 500 participation. You can opt out of optional analytics in Settings > Privacy.
