## The Gaps (By Severity)

### CRITICAL GAPS (Blocking Deals)

#### 1. Trust & Credibility Signals
**What's missing:** SOC 2 audit summary, ISO 27001 cert, GDPR compliance matrix, security architecture, incident response documentation

**Why it matters:** Security teams won't proceed without third-party validation. Compliance officers can't sign contracts without audit documentation.

**Business impact:** 40% of Fortune 500 enterprises won't even schedule a demo without these

**Timeline to fix:** 1-2 weeks (content already exists; just needs publication)

**Quick wins:**
- Publish SOC 2 audit summary
- Add certification badges to homepage
- Create 1-page security overview

---

#### 2. Customer Social Proof
**What's missing:** Customer logos, quantified case studies, executive testimonials, G2/Capterra integration

**Why it matters:** Procurement teams consult peers. "Who else uses this?" is #1 qualification question.

**Business impact:** 35% conversion rate loss vs. competitors with visible customer logos

**Timeline to fix:** 3-4 weeks (requires customer outreach and interviews)

**Quick wins:**
- Contact 5-10 current customers for logo usage
- Collect 3-5 customer testimonials
- Create customer testimonials section on homepage

---

#### 3. Compliance & Legal Documentation
**What's missing:** DPA template, BAA (HIPAA), privacy policy, data retention policies, incident response procedures

**Why it matters:** Legal teams have procurement authority. Missing docs = automatic deal rejection.

**Business impact:** 20-30% of enterprise deals stall at legal review stage (3-6 month delays)

**Timeline to fix:** 2 weeks (legal templates mostly exist; need to publication)

**Quick wins:**
- Publish downloadable DPA template
- Create simple 1-page "Compliance Center" hub
- Link to existing privacy policy prominently

- Create success metrics framework
