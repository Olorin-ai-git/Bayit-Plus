# Campaign Management

Create and manage email and push notification campaigns to engage your Bayit+ audience. Campaigns help promote new content, re-engage inactive users, and communicate important updates.

## Campaign Types

**Email Campaigns**: Rich HTML emails for detailed communications including new release announcements, newsletters, and account updates.

**Push Notifications**: Short, immediate messages delivered to mobile and TV apps for time-sensitive content promotions.

**In-App Messages**: Banners and modals displayed within the app for contextual promotions.

## Creating a Campaign

1. Navigate to **Campaigns** > **Create New**
2. Select the campaign type
3. Define your target audience
4. Design your message content
5. Schedule delivery timing
6. Review and launch

## Audience Targeting

Segment your audience using these criteria:

| Criterion | Examples |
|-----------|----------|
| Activity | Active, inactive, new users |
| Preferences | Genre interests, language |
| Subscription | Free, premium, trial |
| Behavior | Completed series, abandoned |
| Demographics | Age range, location |

Combine multiple criteria for precise targeting.

## Content Personalization

Dynamic fields automatically personalize messages:

- `{{user.name}}`: Recipient's display name
- `{{content.title}}`: Recommended content title
- `{{content.image}}`: Content poster image
- `{{profile.name}}`: Active profile name

## A/B Testing

Test variations to optimize performance:

1. Create two or more message variants
2. Define the test audience percentage
3. Select the success metric (opens, clicks, conversions)
4. Launch the test
5. Review results and deploy the winner

## Campaign Analytics

Track campaign performance with detailed metrics:

- Delivery rate and bounce statistics
- Open rates and click-through rates
- Conversion tracking to content views
- Unsubscribe rates

## Campaign Templates

Save frequently used designs as templates:

1. Design your campaign
2. Click **Save as Template**
3. Name and categorize the template
4. Reuse for future campaigns

Templates maintain consistent branding across communications.
