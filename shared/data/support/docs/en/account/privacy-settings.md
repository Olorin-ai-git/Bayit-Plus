# Privacy Settings

Control how your data is collected, used, and shared within Bayit+.

## Data Privacy Controls

### Accessing Privacy Settings

1. Go to **Settings** > **Account** > **Privacy**
2. Review all privacy options
3. Adjust settings to your preferences
4. Changes take effect immediately

### Data Collection Options

| Setting | Description | Default |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| **Usage Analytics** | Helps improve app performance | On |
| **Personalization** | Improves content recommendations | On |
| **Crash Reports** | Helps fix app issues | On |
| **Marketing Data** | Used for relevant promotions | Off |

### Adjusting Data Collection

1. Go to **Settings** > **Privacy** > **Data Collection**
2. Toggle each option on or off
3. Read the explanation for each setting
4. Confirm your choices

## Watch History Privacy

### Managing Watch History

1. Go to **Settings** > **Privacy** > **Watch History**
2. Choose your preference:
   - **Save History** - Full recommendations
   - **Pause History** - Temporary stop
   - **Delete History** - Remove all records

### Deleting Watch History

To clear your viewing history:
1. Go to **Settings** > **Privacy** > **Watch History**
2. Tap **Delete Watch History**
3. Choose timeframe:
   - Last 24 hours
   - Last 7 days
   - Last 30 days
   - All history
4. Confirm deletion

### Hiding Specific Titles

To remove individual items:
1. Go to your **Watch History**
2. Find the title to remove
3. Swipe left or tap the menu icon
4. Select **Remove from History**

## Sharing Preferences

### Profile Visibility

Control what others in your family can see:
1. Go to **Settings** > **Privacy** > **Profile Visibility**
2. Set your preferences:
   - **Public** - Family members see your activity
   - **Private** - Only you see your activity
   - **Custom** - Choose specific elements

### Activity Sharing

Manage what activity is visible:
- Currently watching status
- Recently watched titles
- Watchlist contents
- Ratings and reviews

### Social Features

1. Go to **Settings** > **Privacy** > **Social**
2. Control social features:
   - Share viewing with friends (Off by default)
   - Show profile in search (Off by default)
   - Allow friend requests (On by default)

## Kids Profile Privacy

### Enhanced Privacy for Children

Kids profiles automatically have:
- Watch history visible only to parents
- No social features enabled
- No marketing data collection
- Restricted content recommendations

### Parental Access

Parents and guardians can:
1. View kids' watch history
2. Set viewing restrictions
3. Approve content additions
4. Monitor screen time

## Data Requests

### Downloading Your Data

1. Go to **Settings** > **Privacy** > **Your Data**
2. Tap **Download My Data**
3. Choose data categories:
   - Account information
   - Watch history
   - Preferences and settings
   - Billing history
4. Receive download link via email within 48 hours

### Deleting Your Data

1. Go to **Settings** > **Privacy** > **Your Data**
2. Tap **Delete My Data**
3. Choose what to delete:
   - Watch history only
   - Recommendations data
   - All personal data (closes account)
4. Confirm with password

### Data Retention

After account deletion:
- Personal data removed within 30 days
- Billing records retained for legal compliance
- Anonymized analytics may be retained
- Backup deletion within 90 days

## Third-Party Services

### Connected Apps

1. Go to **Settings** > **Privacy** > **Connected Apps**
2. View apps with account access
3. Remove apps you no longer use
4. Review permissions for each app

### Advertising Partners

1. Go to **Settings** > **Privacy** > **Advertising**
2. Choose your ad preferences:
   - Personalized ads (based on viewing)
   - General ads (not personalized)
   - No promotional content (Premium only)

## Privacy Questions

For privacy-related inquiries:
1. **In-App:** Settings > Help > Privacy Question
