# Managing Your Subscription

Learn how to manage, upgrade, or cancel your Bayit+ subscription.

## Subscription Plans

### Available Plans

| Plan | Features | Devices | Quality |
|------|----------|---------|---------|
| **Basic** | Live TV, VOD | 1 | SD |
| **Standard** | Live TV, VOD, DVR | 2 | HD |
| **Premium** | All features, Downloads | 4 | 4K + HDR |
| **Family** | All Premium + 6 profiles | 6 | 4K + HDR |

### Viewing Your Current Plan

1. Open Bayit+ app
2. Go to **Settings** > **Account**
3. Tap **Subscription**
4. View current plan and billing date

## Upgrading Your Plan

### How to Upgrade

1. Go to **Settings** > **Subscription**
2. Tap **Change Plan**
3. Select a higher tier
4. Confirm payment method
5. Immediate access to new features

### Upgrade Benefits

When you upgrade:
- Immediate access to new features
- Pro-rated billing for current period
- Higher quality streaming instantly
- More simultaneous devices

## Downgrading Your Plan

### How to Downgrade

1. Go to **Settings** > **Subscription**
2. Tap **Change Plan**
3. Select a lower tier
4. Confirm downgrade
5. Change effective at next billing date

### Downgrade Notes

- Features removed at next billing date
- Downloads may be affected
- Device limit enforced at renewal
- Recorded content preserved

## Billing & Payments

### Payment Methods

We accept:
- Credit/Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- PayPal
- Apple Pay / Google Pay
- Direct carrier billing (select carriers)

### Updating Payment Method

1. Go to **Settings** > **Account**
2. Tap **Payment Method**
3. Add new method or update existing
4. Set as default payment
5. Remove old methods if desired

### Viewing Billing History

1. Go to **Settings** > **Account**
2. Tap **Billing History**
3. View all transactions
4. Download invoices (PDF)

### Failed Payments

If payment fails:
1. Check card expiration
2. Verify billing address
3. Ensure sufficient funds
4. Try different payment method
5. Contact your bank if declined

Grace period: 7 days to update payment before service interruption.

## Cancelling Your Subscription

### How to Cancel

**In App:**
1. Go to **Settings** > **Subscription**
2. Tap **Cancel Subscription**
3. Select reason (optional feedback)
4. Confirm cancellation

**Via App Store (if applicable):**
1. Open App Store/Google Play
2. Go to Subscriptions
3. Find Bayit+
4. Tap Cancel

### After Cancellation

- Access continues until billing period ends
- Downloads remain until expiration
- Profile preferences saved for 1 year
- Can reactivate anytime

### Refund Policy

- Cancel within 7 days for full refund
- No refunds after 7 days
- Contact support for special circumstances
- App store purchases follow store policies

## Pausing Your Subscription

### Pause Options

Not traveling? Pause instead of cancel:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Subscription**
2. Tap **Pause Subscription**
3. Select pause duration (1-3 months)
4. Confirm pause

### While Paused

- No charges during pause
- No access to streaming
- Account preserved
- Auto-resumes after pause period

## Family Sharing

### Adding Family Members

Premium and Family plans support sharing:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Family**
2. Tap **Add Member**
3. Enter their email
4. They'll receive an invitation
5. They create their own profile

### Managing Family Members

- View all family members
- Remove members anytime
- Transfer family ownership
- Each member has own profile/preferences

### Family Restrictions

- All members share same plan
- Payment managed by owner
- Kids profiles can be restricted
- Each member needs own login

## Promotional Offers

### Redeeming Codes

1. Go to **Settings** > **Subscription**
2. Tap **Redeem Code**
3. Enter promo code
4. Discount applied to account

### Bundle Deals

Check if you qualify for bundled pricing:
- Mobile carrier partnerships
- ISP partnerships
- Student discounts
- Annual payment discount

## Subscription FAQs

**Q: When am I billed?**
A: On the same date each month you started.

**Q: Can I change billing date?**
A: Cancel and resubscribe on preferred date.

**Q: Is there a contract?**
A: No, cancel anytime.

**Q: Do prices change?**
A: We provide 30 days notice for price changes.

**Q: What happens to downloads if I cancel?**
A: Downloads expire when subscription ends.

## Contact Billing Support

For billing issues:

1. **In-App:** Settings > Help > Billing Issue
2. **Voice:** "Hey Bayit, I have a billing question"
3. **Email:** billing@bayitplus.com

Include in your message:
- Account email
- Last 4 digits of payment method
- Transaction date if applicable
- Description of issue
