# Torah Content

Access a vast library of Torah learning resources on Bayit+, from daily Daf Yomi to in-depth shiurim on any topic.

## Daf Yomi

Follow the global Daf Yomi cycle with daily shiurim:

1. Navigate to Judaism
2. Select Daf Yomi
3. Choose your preferred maggid shiur
4. Watch or listen to the daily daf

Multiple teachers are available in English, Hebrew, and Yiddish.

## Weekly Parasha

Prepare for Shabbat with Parasha content:

- Video shiurim on the weekly portion
- Audio classes for on-the-go learning
- Written summaries and commentary
- Children's Parasha content for families

New content is added each week before Shabbat.

## Topic-Based Learning

Explore Torah topics that interest you:

- Chumash with classical commentaries
- Gemara and Mishnah courses
- Halacha for practical application
- Jewish philosophy and thought
- Chassidut and Mussar

Use the search feature to find shiurim on specific subjects.

## Featured Rabbis

Follow your favorite rabbis and educators:

1. Visit a rabbi's profile page
2. Tap Follow
3. Their new content appears in your feed
4. Receive notifications when they post

## Learning Playlists

Create custom learning playlists:

- Add shiurim to playlists for later
- Organize by topic or teacher
- Share playlists with friends
- Track your progress through series

## Download for Offline Learning

Download shiurim to learn without internet:

1. Find the shiur you want
2. Tap the download icon
3. Access downloaded content in your Library
4. Listen or watch anywhere

Downloads remain available for 30 days.
