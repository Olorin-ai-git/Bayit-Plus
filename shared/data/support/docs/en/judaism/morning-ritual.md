# Morning Ritual

The Morning Ritual in Bayit+ provides a personalized daily experience combining practical information with Jewish content.

## Starting Your Morning

To access the Morning Ritual:

1. Open Bayit+ in the morning
2. The ritual launches automatically if enabled
3. Or select Morning Ritual from the Judaism section

The experience is designed to take just a few minutes.

## Weather Information

Begin your day with local weather:

- Current temperature and conditions
- Daily high and low forecast
- Precipitation probability
- Hourly breakdown for the day ahead

Weather data uses your location for accurate local conditions.

## Daily News

Stay informed with curated news:

- Top headlines from trusted sources
- Jewish community news
- Israel news updates
- Customizable news categories

Select your preferred news sources in settings.

## Jewish Daily Content

Each morning includes Jewish inspiration:

- Hebrew date and upcoming events
- Parasha or holiday connection
- Daily learning segment
- Inspirational quote or thought

Content rotates daily to keep each morning fresh.

## Zmanim Overview

Key times for the day are displayed:

- Sunrise and sunset
- Latest Shema time
- Candle lighting if Friday
- Any special observances

Tap any time to see the full zmanim list.

## Customizing Your Ritual

Personalize the morning experience:

1. Go to Settings
2. Select Morning Ritual
3. Choose which sections to include
4. Set the order of content
5. Adjust the auto-launch time

You can enable or disable any section based on your preferences.

## Audio Mode

Listen to your morning ritual while getting ready:

- All content is available in audio format
- Voice reads weather, news, and learning
- Hands-free experience for busy mornings

Enable audio mode in the ritual settings or tap the speaker icon when starting.
