# Live Radio on Bayit+

Stream live radio stations directly through the Bayit+ app. Enjoy music, news, talk shows, and more from stations around the world.

## Accessing Radio

1. Open the Bayit+ app on any device
2. Select "Radio" from the main navigation menu
3. Browse available stations or search for specific ones
4. Tap a station to begin live streaming

## Station Categories

Radio stations are organized by genre and format for easy discovery:
- **Music**: Pop, Rock, Classical, Jazz, Electronic, and more
- **News**: 24-hour news stations and current affairs
- **Talk**: Discussion, interviews, and call-in programs
- **Sports**: Live sports coverage and analysis
- **Cultural**: Ethnic and international programming
- **Religious**: Faith-based content and services

## Radio Features

### Now Playing
View the currently playing song or program with artist information and album artwork when available.

### Station Favorites
Mark stations as favorites for quick access. Your favorite stations appear at the top of your radio list.

### Recently Played
Access your listening history to quickly return to stations you have enjoyed.

### Background Listening
Radio playback continues when you navigate to other areas of the app or minimize on mobile devices.

## Listening on the Go

### Mobile Devices
Stream radio on iOS and Android with background playback support.

### CarPlay and Android Auto
Access your favorite radio stations while driving. Controls integrate with your vehicle's infotainment system.

## Audio Quality

Bayit+ radio streams adapt to your network conditions to ensure uninterrupted playback. Higher quality streams are delivered when bandwidth allows.

## Station Requests

Have a station you would like to see on Bayit+? Submit suggestions through the feedback option in your account settings.
