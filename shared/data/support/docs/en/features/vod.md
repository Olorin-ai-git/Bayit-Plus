# Video on Demand

Access thousands of movies and TV shows with Bayit+ On Demand.

## Browsing Content

### Home Screen
Your personalized home screen shows:
- **Continue Watching** - Resume where you left off
- **Recommended for You** - Based on your preferences
- **Trending Now** - Popular content this week
- **New Releases** - Recently added titles
- **Categories** - Browse by genre

### Categories
Explore content organized by type:
- Action & Adventure
- Comedy
- Drama
- Documentary
- Horror & Thriller
- Kids & Family
- Romance
- Sci-Fi & Fantasy

### Search
Find specific content quickly:
1. Tap the search icon
2. Type or use voice search
3. Filter by type (Movies/Series)
4. Sort by relevance, date, or rating

## Watching Content

### Starting Playback
1. Select a title to view details
2. Read synopsis, cast, and ratings
3. Tap **Play** to start
4. Choose an episode (for series)

### Playback Controls
- **Play/Pause** - Tap center or press space
- **Seek** - Drag the progress bar
- **Skip** - Jump 10 seconds forward/back
- **Volume** - Swipe on right side or use keys
- **Subtitles** - Tap CC icon
- **Audio Track** - Choose language or commentary
- **Quality** - Adjust streaming quality
- **Speed** - Watch at 0.5x to 2x speed

### Subtitles & Audio
Multiple language options available:
1. Tap the **CC** button during playback
2. Choose subtitle language
3. Select audio track
4. Adjust subtitle appearance in Settings

## Managing Your Library

### My List
Save content to watch later:
1. Open any title
2. Tap **Add to My List**
3. Access from the **My List** tab

### Downloads (Premium)
Download content for offline viewing:
1. Open a title's details page
2. Tap **Download**
3. Choose quality (Standard/High)
4. Access from **Downloads** tab

Download limits:
- Up to 25 titles at once
- Downloads expire after 30 days
- Watched downloads expire after 48 hours

### Watch History
View and manage your history:
1. Go to **Profile** > **Watch History**
2. See all watched content
3. Remove items or clear history
4. History syncs across devices

## Content Ratings

We use standard content ratings:

| Rating | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| G | General Audiences |
| PG | Parental Guidance |
| PG-13 | Parents Cautioned |
| R | Restricted |
| TV-MA | Mature Audiences |

Enable parental controls in Settings to restrict access by rating.

## Smart Recommendations

Bayit+ learns your preferences to suggest content you'll love:

- Based on watch history
- Similar to titles you've liked
- Popular in your region
- Trending content
- New releases in your favorite genres

### Improving Recommendations
- Rate content with thumbs up/down
- Add items to your list
- Complete your profile preferences
- Watch a variety of content

## Series Features

### Episode Navigation
- View all seasons and episodes
- See episode descriptions
- Track watched episodes
- Auto-play next episode

### Binge Mode
Enable continuous playback:
1. Start watching a series
2. Next episode auto-plays after credits
3. Skip intro option available
4. Turn off in playback settings

## Troubleshooting

### Content Won't Play
1. Check your subscription status
2. Verify internet connection
3. Try a different title
4. Clear app cache

### Missing Subtitles
1. Not all content has all languages
2. Check available options in CC menu
3. Report missing subtitles via feedback

See [Streaming Issues](/troubleshooting/streaming-issues) for more help.
