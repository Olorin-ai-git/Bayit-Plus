# Podcasts on Bayit+

Discover and enjoy a diverse collection of podcasts directly within the Bayit+ app. From educational content to entertainment, our podcast library offers something for every listener.

## Finding Podcasts

### Browse by Category
Navigate to the Podcasts section from the main menu to explore content organized by topic:
- News and Current Events
- Entertainment and Culture
- Education and Learning
- Health and Wellness
- Technology
- Comedy
- True Crime
- Family and Kids

### Featured Collections
Our editorial team curates special podcast collections highlighting trending shows, new releases, and themed content for seasons and events.

### Search
Use the search function to find specific podcasts by name, host, or topic. Voice search is available on supported devices.

## Listening to Podcasts

1. Select a podcast from the library
2. View the episode list and show description
3. Tap any episode to begin playback
4. Use playback controls to pause, skip, or adjust speed

## Playback Features

- **Variable Speed**: Adjust playback from 0.5x to 2x speed
- **Sleep Timer**: Set automatic stop after a duration
- **Skip Controls**: Jump forward or back in 15-second increments
- **Background Playback**: Continue listening while using other apps
- **CarPlay/Android Auto**: Stream podcasts during your commute

## Managing Your Podcasts

### Subscribe to Shows
Follow your favorite podcasts to receive notifications when new episodes are released.

### Download for Offline
Premium subscribers can download episodes for offline listening on mobile devices.

### Listening History
Access your recently played episodes from your profile to resume where you left off.

## Podcast Recommendations
Bayit+ learns your preferences and suggests new podcasts based on your listening history and interests.
