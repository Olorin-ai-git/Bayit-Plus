# Friends

Connect with family and friends on Bayit+ to share your viewing experience and discover new content together.

## Adding Friends

To add a friend on Bayit+:

1. Navigate to your Profile
2. Select the Friends tab
3. Tap Add Friend
4. Search by username or email address
5. Send a friend request

Your friend will receive a notification and can accept or decline your request.

## Managing Friend Requests

View pending friend requests in your Profile under the Friends tab. You can:

- Accept requests to add someone to your friends list
- Decline requests you do not wish to accept
- Cancel outgoing requests you have sent

## Friend Activity

Stay connected with what your friends are watching:

- See recently watched shows and movies on their profiles
- View their favorite content and recommendations
- Get notified when friends start watching live content

## Privacy Settings

Control what friends can see about your activity:

1. Go to Settings
2. Select Privacy
3. Choose your activity visibility preferences

Options include showing your watch history to friends, hiding specific content from your activity feed, or appearing offline.

## Removing Friends

To remove someone from your friends list:

1. Go to your Profile
2. Select the Friends tab
3. Find the friend you want to remove
4. Tap the menu icon and select Remove Friend

The person will not be notified when you remove them from your friends list.
