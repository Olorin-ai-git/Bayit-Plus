# Watch Parties

Watch Parties let you enjoy movies and shows together with friends in real-time, no matter where you are.

## Starting a Watch Party

To host a Watch Party:

1. Select the content you want to watch
2. Tap the Watch Party icon on the player screen
3. Choose Start Watch Party
4. Invite friends from your friends list
5. Wait for guests to join, then press Play

Everyone in the party will see the same content at the same moment.

## Joining a Watch Party

When a friend invites you to a Watch Party:

1. You will receive a notification
2. Tap the notification or find the invite in your messages
3. Select Join Party
4. Your video will sync automatically with the host

## Chat Features

During a Watch Party, you can communicate with other viewers:

- Text chat appears on the side of the screen
- Send reactions that appear on screen for everyone
- Use voice chat for live conversation during the watch

Toggle chat visibility using the chat icon on the player controls.

## Host Controls

As the host, you control the playback for everyone:

- Pause and resume playback for all viewers
- Rewind or fast-forward with automatic sync
- Remove participants if needed
- End the party at any time

## Watch Party Etiquette

For the best experience:

- Ensure stable internet connection before starting
- Use headphones if using voice chat
- Avoid spoilers in chat for first-time viewers
- Respect the host's control of playback

Watch Parties support up to 10 participants per session.
