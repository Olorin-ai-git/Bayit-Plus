# Chess

Challenge your friends to a game of chess directly within Bayit+. Play live matches while enjoying your favorite content.

## Starting a Game

To start a chess game:

1. Open the Games section from the main menu
2. Select Chess
3. Choose Challenge a Friend
4. Select a friend from your list
5. Set your preferred time control
6. Send the challenge

Your friend will receive a notification and can accept to begin the match.

## Game Controls

During a chess game:

- Tap a piece to select it
- Tap a highlighted square to move
- Use the clock display to track time
- Press the flag icon to resign
- Press the handshake icon to offer a draw

## Time Controls

Several time formats are available:

- Bullet: 1 minute per player
- Blitz: 5 minutes per player
- Rapid: 15 minutes per player
- Classical: 30 minutes per player
- Unlimited: No time restriction

Select your preferred time control when creating a game.

## Game History

View your past games in the Chess section:

- Review completed matches move by move
- See your win/loss record
- Analyze positions with move suggestions
- Share interesting games with friends

## Playing While Watching

You can play chess while watching content:

1. Start your video or live stream
2. Open Chess from the quick menu
3. The game appears in a compact overlay
4. Switch focus between the game and video

This feature works on all devices with picture-in-picture support.

## Rankings

Your chess rating updates after each game. View the leaderboard to see how you rank among friends and the broader Bayit+ community.
