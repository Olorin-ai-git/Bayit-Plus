# Live TV Guide

Stream hundreds of live channels with Bayit+ Live TV.

## Accessing Live TV

1. Open the Bayit+ app
2. Navigate to the **Live** tab
3. Browse or search for channels
4. Tap a channel to start watching

## Channel Guide

The channel guide shows what's currently playing and upcoming programs.

### Using the Guide
- **Current Programs** - See what's on now across all channels
- **Schedule View** - View upcoming programs for the next 7 days
- **Filter by Category** - Sports, News, Movies, Entertainment, Kids
- **Search Channels** - Find specific channels by name or number

### Guide Navigation
- Swipe left/right to move through time
- Swipe up/down to browse channels
- Tap a program for details
- Long press to set a reminder

## Live TV Features

### Picture-in-Picture
Watch live TV while browsing other content:
1. Start watching a channel
2. Press the PiP button
3. Continue browsing in the app

### Mini Player
A small player appears when you navigate away, letting you keep watching.

### Channel Favorites
Star your favorite channels for quick access:
1. Open channel details
2. Tap the star icon
3. Access favorites from the Favorites tab

### Channel Recording (Premium)
Record live TV to watch later:
1. Open the program guide
2. Find the show you want to record
3. Tap **Record**
4. Access recordings from your library

## Quality Settings

Adjust streaming quality based on your connection:

| Setting | Resolution | Data Usage |
|---------|------------|------------|
| Auto | Adaptive | Varies |
| Low | 480p | ~0.5 GB/hr |
| Medium | 720p | ~1.5 GB/hr |
| High | 1080p | ~3 GB/hr |
| Ultra | 4K | ~7 GB/hr |

## Parental Controls

Restrict access to certain channels or content ratings:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Parental Controls**
2. Set a PIN code
3. Choose restricted channels or ratings
4. Save your settings

## Troubleshooting

### Channel Won't Load
1. Check your internet connection
2. Try lowering the quality setting
3. Close and reopen the app
4. Restart your device

### Poor Quality
1. Test your internet speed
2. Connect to Wi-Fi if on mobile data
3. Reduce quality in settings
4. Move closer to your router

### Guide Not Updating
1. Pull down to refresh
2. Check your internet connection
3. Clear app cache
4. Update the app

See [Streaming Issues](/troubleshooting/streaming-issues) for more help.
