# Offline Downloads

Download movies, TV episodes, and podcasts to watch without an internet connection. This feature is perfect for travel, commutes, or areas with limited connectivity.

## Device Availability

Offline downloads are available exclusively on mobile devices:
- **iOS**: iPhone and iPad
- **Android**: Phones and tablets

Downloads are not available on web browsers, Apple TV, or Android TV.

## Subscription Requirements

Download limits vary by subscription tier:
- **Basic**: Downloads not included
- **Standard**: Up to 10 titles at a time
- **Premium**: Unlimited downloads
- **Family**: Unlimited downloads per profile

## How to Download

1. Find the movie, episode, or podcast you want to download
2. Look for the download icon (arrow pointing down)
3. Tap the icon to begin downloading
4. Monitor progress in the Downloads section

## Managing Downloads

### Viewing Downloaded Content
Access your downloads from the "Downloads" tab in the app menu. Content is organized by type and sorted by download date.

### Storage Information
View how much device storage your downloads are using in the Downloads settings. Bayit+ displays:
- Total space used
- Space available on device
- Size of individual downloads

### Removing Downloads
Free up space by removing downloaded content:
1. Go to Downloads
2. Swipe left on the item (iOS) or long-press (Android)
3. Select "Remove Download"

Content remains in your library for streaming after removal.

## Download Settings

Configure your download preferences:
- **Quality**: Choose between Standard (smaller files) and High (better quality)
- **Wi-Fi Only**: Restrict downloads to Wi-Fi connections to save mobile data
- **Auto-Download**: Automatically download new episodes of subscribed series

## Expiration

Downloaded content has an expiration period based on licensing agreements. The app displays remaining time for each download. Once expired, content must be re-downloaded while connected to the internet.

## Troubleshooting

If downloads fail, verify you have sufficient storage space and a stable internet connection. Some content may have temporary download restrictions due to licensing.
