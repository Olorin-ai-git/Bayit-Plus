# DVR Recordings

Record live TV programs to watch on your schedule. The Bayit+ DVR feature lets you never miss your favorite shows, even when you cannot watch them live.

## Recording Requirements

DVR functionality is available on Standard, Premium, and Family subscription plans. Storage limits vary by plan:
- **Standard**: 25 hours of recording storage
- **Premium**: 100 hours of recording storage
- **Family**: 100 hours of recording storage

## Scheduling a Recording

### From the Program Guide
1. Open the Electronic Program Guide (EPG)
2. Navigate to the show you want to record
3. Select the program to view details
4. Tap "Record" to schedule a single recording
5. For series, select "Record Series" to capture all episodes

### From Search Results
1. Search for a show by title
2. Select the program from results
3. View upcoming air times
4. Choose "Record" on any scheduled broadcast

## Managing Recordings

### Viewing Your Library
Access all recordings from the "My Recordings" section in your account menu. Recordings are organized by:
- Recently recorded
- Series recordings
- Movies
- Sports and events

### Storage Management
When storage is full, Bayit+ notifies you before scheduled recordings. You can:
- Delete watched recordings
- Remove individual episodes from series
- Upgrade your plan for additional storage

### Recording Settings
Configure default recording behavior:
- Start recording early (1-5 minutes buffer)
- Extend recording time (for live events)
- Keep recordings for a specified duration
- Automatic deletion of watched content

## Watching Recordings

Recordings are available to stream on all devices linked to your account. Features include:
- Skip commercials with fast-forward
- Pause and resume playback
- Continue watching across devices

## Recording Conflicts

When two or more programs overlap, Bayit+ alerts you to the conflict. You can choose which program takes priority or upgrade your plan for additional simultaneous recording capacity.
