# Troubleshooting Payment Issues

Having trouble with payments, billing, or subscription charges? This guide helps resolve common payment problems.

## Payment Declined

### Common Decline Reasons

| Reason | Solution |
|--------|----------|
| Insufficient funds | Add funds to account |
| Card expired | Update card details |
| Incorrect details | Re-enter card information |
| Bank security block | Contact your bank |
| International restriction | Enable international purchases |

### Resolving Declined Payments

1. Go to **Settings** > **Account** > **Payment Method**
2. Verify card details are correct
3. Check expiration date is current
4. Confirm billing address matches bank records
5. Try payment again

### Contacting Your Bank

If payment continues to fail:
1. Call your bank's support line
2. Confirm the card is active
3. Ask about blocked transactions
4. Request authorization for Bayit+
5. Some banks require SMS confirmation

### Using Alternative Payment

If your card does not work:
1. Go to **Settings** > **Account** > **Payment Method**
2. Tap **Add Payment Method**
3. Try PayPal, Apple Pay, or Google Pay
4. Or use a different card

## Billing Errors

### Unexpected Charges

If you notice an unfamiliar charge:
1. Go to **Settings** > **Account** > **Billing History**
2. Review recent transactions
3. Check for plan upgrades
4. Verify no additional purchases

### Common Charge Explanations

| Charge Type | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| Monthly subscription | Regular plan charge |
| Plan upgrade | Pro-rated upgrade cost |
| Add-on purchase | Premium channels or features |
| Reactivation | After pause period ends |

### Disputing a Charge

If you believe a charge is incorrect:
1. Document the transaction details
2. Go to **Settings** > **Help** > **Billing Issue**
3. Select **Dispute a Charge**
4. Provide transaction date and amount
5. Explain why you believe it is incorrect

### Refund Policy

- Within 7 days of charge: Full refund available
- After 7 days: Case-by-case review
- App store purchases: Follow store refund process
- Contact support for special circumstances

## Subscription Issues

### Subscription Not Active

If your subscription shows inactive:
1. Go to **Settings** > **Account** > **Subscription**
2. Check subscription status
3. Verify payment was successful
4. Review billing history for failed payments

### Reactivating Subscription

1. Go to **Settings** > **Subscription**
2. Tap **Reactivate**
3. Verify payment method
4. Confirm subscription plan
5. Access restored immediately

### Subscription Shows Wrong Plan

If you have the wrong subscription tier:
1. Check recent plan changes in billing history
2. Verify upgrade or downgrade confirmation
3. Contact support with payment receipt
4. Plan sync may take up to 15 minutes

### Duplicate Subscriptions

If charged multiple times:
1. Check if subscribed through multiple stores
2. Review Apple App Store subscriptions
3. Review Google Play subscriptions
4. Cancel duplicate subscriptions
5. Request refund for duplicates

## App Store Billing

### Managing iOS Subscription

1. Open device Settings
2. Tap your Apple ID
3. Tap Subscriptions
4. Find Bayit+
5. Manage or cancel subscription

### Managing Android Subscription

1. Open Google Play Store
2. Tap Menu > Subscriptions
3. Find Bayit+
4. Manage or cancel subscription

### App Store Refunds

**Apple:**
1. Go to reportaproblem.apple.com
2. Sign in with Apple ID
3. Find the Bayit+ charge
4. Request refund

**Google:**
1. Go to play.google.com/store/account
2. Find purchase history
3. Select the charge
4. Request refund

## Payment Method Management

### Adding a Payment Method

1. Go to **Settings** > **Account** > **Payment Method**
2. Tap **Add Payment Method**
3. Choose type: Card, PayPal, etc.
4. Enter details
5. Verify with small authorization charge

### Updating Card Details

1. Go to **Settings** > **Account** > **Payment Method**
2. Select the card to update
3. Tap **Edit**
4. Update expiration date or address
5. Save changes

### Removing a Payment Method

1. Go to **Settings** > **Account** > **Payment Method**
2. Select the method to remove
3. Tap **Remove**
4. Confirm removal
5. Ensure another method is set as default

### Setting Default Payment

1. Go to **Settings** > **Account** > **Payment Method**
2. Select preferred method
3. Tap **Set as Default**
4. Future charges use this method

## Failed Payment Recovery

### Grace Period

After a failed payment:
- 7-day grace period before service interruption
- Multiple retry attempts during this period
- Email notifications sent about failed payment
- Update payment method to avoid interruption

### Service Interrupted

If your service was interrupted:
1. Update payment method
2. Pay outstanding balance
3. Service restores immediately
4. No late fees applied

## Contact Support

For billing assistance:
1. **In-App:** Settings > Help > Billing Issue
2. **Email:** billing@bayitplus.com
3. **Voice:** "Hey Bayit, I have a billing question"
4. Include: account email, last 4 digits of payment, and issue description
