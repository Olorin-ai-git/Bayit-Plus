# Device Compatibility

Verify your device meets the requirements to run Bayit+ and enjoy all features.

## Mobile Devices

### iPhone and iPad

| Requirement | Minimum |
|-------------|---------|
| iOS Version | iOS 14.0 or later |
| iPadOS Version | iPadOS 14.0 or later |
| Storage | 100 MB free (app only) |
| Storage with downloads | 2 GB+ recommended |

**Supported Models:**
- iPhone 6s and newer
- iPad Air 2 and newer
- iPad mini 4 and newer
- iPad Pro (all models)

### Android Devices

| Requirement | Minimum |
|-------------|---------|
| Android Version | Android 8.0 (Oreo) or later |
| RAM | 2 GB minimum |
| Storage | 100 MB free (app only) |
| Google Play Services | Required |

**Supported Devices:**
- Most Android phones from 2018 onward
- Most Android tablets from 2018 onward
- Devices with ARM or ARM64 processors

## Checking Your Device

### Verifying Compatibility

1. Check your device settings for OS version
2. Compare against requirements above
3. Update OS if below minimum
4. Download Bayit+ from your app store

### Device Not Listed

If your device is not listed:
- It may still work if it meets requirements
- Try downloading the app to test
- Check our website for latest compatibility list
- Contact support for specific device questions

## Updating Your Device

### iOS/iPadOS

1. Go to Settings > General > Software Update
2. Download and install available updates
3. Restart device after update

### Android

1. Go to Settings > System > Software Update
2. Check for updates
3. Download and install if available

### Smart TV

1. Go to TV Settings > System > Software Update
2. Check for updates
3. Follow on-screen instructions

## Contact Support

For compatibility questions:
1. **In-App:** Settings > Help > Device Compatibility
2. **Web:** bayitplus.com/devices
3. Include your device model and OS version
