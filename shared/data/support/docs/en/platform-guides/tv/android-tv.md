# Android TV Setup

Enjoy Bayit+ on your Android TV or Google TV device with full integration into your smart TV experience.

## Installation

1. Open the **Google Play Store** on your Android TV
2. Navigate to Search and enter **Bayit+**
3. Select the app from the results
4. Click **Install** and wait for download to complete
5. Open Bayit+ from your Apps row

## Supported Devices

- Android TV devices running Android TV 9.0 or later
- Google TV devices (Chromecast with Google TV, Sony, TCL, etc.)
- NVIDIA Shield TV
- Xiaomi Mi Box
- Other certified Android TV devices

## Signing In

### Using Your Phone
1. Open Bayit+ on your TV
2. Select "Sign in with Phone"
3. On your phone, visit the URL shown on screen
4. Enter the code displayed on your TV

### Using Remote
1. Select "Sign in with Email"
2. Use the D-pad to enter your email and password
3. Navigate to "Sign In" and press OK

## Google TV Integration

### Watchlist
Add content to your Google TV Watchlist to access it from the home screen.

### Recommendations
Bayit+ content appears in your personalized For You tab based on your viewing history.

### Google Assistant
Press the Assistant button on your remote:
- "Show me comedies on Bayit+"
- "Open Bayit+"
- "Play the next episode"
- "Rewind 2 minutes"

## Remote Control Basics

| Button | Action |
|--------|--------|
| D-pad | Navigate menus |
| OK/Select | Choose item or play |
| Back | Return to previous screen |
| Home | Return to Android TV home |
| Play/Pause | Control playback |

## Features

### Multiple Profiles
Each family member can have their own profile with personalized recommendations and watch history.

### Continue Watching
Your progress syncs automatically across all your devices signed into the same account.

## Tips

- Use the Google TV app on your phone as a remote for easier navigation
- Pin Bayit+ to your home screen favorites for quick access
- Enable developer options to check network performance if experiencing buffering
