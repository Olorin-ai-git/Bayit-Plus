# CarPlay Setup

Listen to Bayit+ audio content safely while driving with Apple CarPlay integration.

## Requirements

- iPhone with iOS 15.0 or later
- CarPlay-compatible vehicle or aftermarket head unit
- Bayit+ app installed on your iPhone

## Connecting to CarPlay

### Wired Connection
1. Connect your iPhone to your car's USB port using a Lightning cable
2. CarPlay will activate automatically
3. The Bayit+ icon appears on your CarPlay home screen

### Wireless Connection
1. Press and hold the voice control button on your steering wheel
2. Enable Bluetooth and Wi-Fi on your iPhone
3. Go to Settings > General > CarPlay on your iPhone
4. Select your vehicle and enable wireless CarPlay

## Using Bayit+ with CarPlay

### Starting Audio Content
1. Tap the Bayit+ icon on your CarPlay display
2. Browse through audio categories
3. Select a podcast, audiobook, or audio program
4. Playback begins automatically

### Available Content
- Podcasts
- Torah lectures and shiurim
- Audiobooks
- Audio programs
- Music playlists

### Playback Controls
- Use steering wheel controls for play/pause and skip
- Adjust volume using your car's volume controls
- Tap the screen to access additional options

## Siri Commands While Driving

Keep your hands on the wheel with voice commands:
- "Hey Siri, play my podcast on Bayit+"
- "Hey Siri, skip this episode"
- "Hey Siri, pause"
- "Hey Siri, resume playback"

## Safety Features

- Video content is disabled while driving
- Only audio content is accessible through CarPlay
- Large, easy-to-tap buttons for minimal distraction
- Voice control is the recommended interaction method

## Tips

- Queue content before starting your drive
- Download episodes for areas with poor cellular coverage
- Use Do Not Disturb While Driving for fewer interruptions
