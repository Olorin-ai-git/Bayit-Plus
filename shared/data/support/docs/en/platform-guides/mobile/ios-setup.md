# iOS App Setup

Get started with Bayit+ on your iPhone or iPad and enjoy streaming with powerful iOS features.

## Installation

1. Open the **App Store** on your iOS device
2. Search for **Bayit+**
3. Tap **Get** to download and install
4. Open the app and sign in with your account

## System Requirements

- iOS 15.0 or later
- iPhone 8 or newer, iPad (6th generation) or newer
- 150 MB free storage space

## Key Features

### Siri Integration
Control Bayit+ hands-free with Siri commands:
- "Hey Siri, play something on Bayit+"
- "Hey Siri, pause Bayit+"
- "Hey Siri, open Bayit+"

### AirPlay Streaming
Stream content to your Apple TV or AirPlay-compatible devices:
1. Start playing content in the app
2. Tap the AirPlay icon in the player
3. Select your AirPlay device from the list

### Picture-in-Picture (PiP)
Continue watching while using other apps:
1. Start playing a video
2. Swipe up or press Home to minimize
3. The video continues in a floating window
4. Tap to return to full screen

### Offline Downloads
Download content to watch without internet:
1. Find the content you want to download
2. Tap the download icon
3. Select video quality
4. Access downloads from your Library tab

## Settings and Preferences

- **Video Quality:** Adjust streaming quality in Settings > Playback
- **Cellular Data:** Control data usage in Settings > Downloads
- **Notifications:** Manage alerts in Settings > Notifications

## Tips

- Enable Face ID or Touch ID for quick sign-in
- Use Dark Mode for comfortable nighttime viewing
- Connect AirPods for automatic audio switching
